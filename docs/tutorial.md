# StockArithm Tutorial

This is the plain-English guide for the public site.

## What this is

StockArithm is a public paper-trading lab for alternative data signals.

It turns non-traditional inputs into trading ideas, tests them, and publishes the results.

Some signals work. Some fail. We keep both visible.

## Where to start

1. Read the homepage.
2. Look at the leaderboard.
3. Check the daily report.
4. Browse the signals index.
5. Read the families page if you want to understand the buckets.

## What each page means

### Home

The homepage is the short explanation.

It shows:
- what the lab is
- the chart
- the current state of the lab
- quick links to the important pages

### Leaderboard

The leaderboard is the live scorecard.

It shows:
- which signals are beating SPY
- which ones are flat
- which ones are underwater
- which ones are too thin to trust yet

### Signals

The signals page is the inventory.

It lists all signals without giving away the scoreboard.

Use it when you want to know what exists.

### Families

Families are groups of signals that try to measure the same general thing.

Examples:
- travel and mobility
- consumer demand
- market mechanics
- sector rotation

### Daily Report

The daily report tells you what changed after the nightly run.

Use it when you want the freshest snapshot.

### Premium Preview

This shows what the deeper pages will look like later.

It is not the public product.

### Legal

This is the plain disclaimer page.

It tells you the site is experimental and not financial advice.

### Biscotti

Biscotti is the mascot signal.

It is a contrarian monthly sector rotation rule and the page exists because it has earned a full story.

## How to read the key terms

### SPY

SPY is the benchmark.

If a signal is beating SPY, it is doing better than the market proxy we use.

### Drawdown

Drawdown is how far the equity curve falls from a prior peak.

It tells you how ugly the ride was.

### Sharpe ratio

Sharpe is a rough measure of return compared with volatility.

Higher is generally better, but it is not magic.

### Live-only

Live-only means the signal is running in public but has not been backtested into a clean historical story.

### Promoted

Promoted means the signal has earned a stronger public slot.

### Parked

Parked means the signal is not currently active.

### Graveyard

Graveyard means the signal failed enough that we keep it visible as a dead end.

## What to look for

- If the leaderboard is mostly underwater, that is not a bug.
- If a page looks sparse, that usually means the signal is thin or early.
- If a label says there is no clean comparator, that means we do not have an apples-to-apples baseline.
- If the lab looks rough, that is the honest state of the lab.

## What not to expect

- Do not expect every signal to win.
- Do not expect the site to hide failures.
- Do not expect the public pages to give away the actual ranking edge.

## The one-sentence version

StockArithm is a public lab for alternative data signals, and the point is to show what is working, what is failing, and why.
