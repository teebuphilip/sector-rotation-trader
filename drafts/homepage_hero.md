# Stockarithm Homepage Hero Draft

## Chosen Hero

### Headline
Weird market signals, run in public.

### Subheadline
Stockarithm tracks paper-traded algos against SPY. The wins, misses, idle names, and dead signals all stay visible.

### Primary CTA
See the public leaderboard

### Secondary CTA
Get weekly lab notes

### Proof Strip
- {total} algos tracked
- {signal_count} public signals
- Paper-traded only
- Failures stay visible

## Why This Is The Right Hero

This avoids pretending to be an AI stock picker. It says exactly what the product is: a public signal lab with receipts.

The conversion job is not to make someone buy immediately. The job is to make a stranger understand the experiment fast enough to click the leaderboard or join the weekly lab notes.

## Backup Variant

### Headline
A public lab for weird trading signals.

### Subheadline
Every signal is paper-tracked against SPY. Winners stay visible. Failures stay visible. The whole point is the record.
