## Style Notes

- **Shorter than Reddit** — each line is a beat
- **Personality-driven** — Biscotti, "my actual dog", self-deprecating
- **Transparent** — lead with losses, graveyard, failures
- **Visual-first** — mention screenshots/charts when possible
- **No hashtags** unless genuinely useful (#algotrading, #fintwit sparingly)
- **Link in last line or bio reference**
