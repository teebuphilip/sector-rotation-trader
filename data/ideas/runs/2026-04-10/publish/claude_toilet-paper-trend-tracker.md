# Toilet Paper Trend Tracker

**Idea ID:** `toilet-paper-trend-tracker`
**Source:** anthropic / claude-3-haiku-20240307
**Frequency:** daily

## Thesis
By monitoring real-time data on toilet paper purchases and supply chain indicators, we can identify emerging demand trends and position ourselves ahead of potential shortages or surges.

## Universe
- CL
- SWK
- KMB

## Data Sources
- Google Trends
- Retail sales data from the U.S. Census Bureau
- Shipping and logistics data from the U.S. Bureau of Transportation Statistics

## Signal Logic
Monitor Google Trends data for toilet paper-related search queries. Identify significant spikes in search volume compared to historical trends. Cross-reference with retail sales data to confirm changes in consumer demand. Also track shipping and logistics metrics like truck loadings and warehouse inventories to gauge supply chain health.

## Entry / Exit
Buy positions in companies that manufacture or distribute toilet paper when search trends and sales data indicate an impending surge in demand. Sell when the data indicates demand has normalized.

## Position Sizing
Size positions based on the magnitude of the demand signal. Larger spikes in search volume and sales warrant larger position sizes.

## Risks
Potential for false signals due to seasonal or one-off events unrelated to true demand. Supply chain data may not be timely enough to capture rapid changes in the market.

## Implementation Notes
Develop a dashboard to monitor the relevant data sources, set thresholds for buy/sell signals, and automate position sizing and trading. Test the strategy on historical data to fine-tune the signal logic.
